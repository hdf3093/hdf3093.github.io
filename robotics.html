adafruit Circuit Playground Express Robot with Ultrasonic Sensor

Designed to go through an obstacle course, this robot is programmed to move forward and recalibrate once it hits an obstacle it cannot move past. It hits the obstacle, moves backwards, pauses, and turns either left or right while moving forward slightly to gauge its path.
