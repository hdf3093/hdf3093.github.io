I am originally from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I’m based in Brooklyn, New York and an incoming third-year at New York University. I’m an interdisciplinary student studying computer programming, humane technology design, robotics, and artificial intelligence. I am also learning Japanese in preparation for my semester-long studies at Waseda University in Tokyo, Japan, where I will be studying computer science and communications engineering at the Faculty of Science and Engineering. I’ve been fascinated with language acquisition and human linguistics since I’ve existed; I’m originally from Buenos Aires, Argentina and was raised speaking Spanish and English natively thanks to my language-loving parents who both studied to become English teachers in Argentina. I also am interested in surveillance capitalism, learning new softwares, and design.

Whether technological or natural, language, in its endless fruition and perpetuation, is persistently fascinating. I’ve enjoyed what I have been able to focus on greatly. My intention in creating this working index is to publish/archive/come-back-to my projects and musings, on the web.
